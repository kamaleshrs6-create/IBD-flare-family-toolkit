Daily Symptom Monitoring

Regular symptom tracking helps your child's IBD team detect flare-ups early, assess treatment response, and make informed decisions during clinic visits.

The tracker records important symptoms including:

  • Abdominal pain
  • Stool frequency
  • Blood in stool
  • Nocturnal stools
  • Urgency
  • Appetite
  • Weight changes
  • Energy level
  • Fever
  • Medication adherence
  • General wellbeing
Open Symptom Tracker

Why Track Symptoms?

  • Detect flares earlier
  • Monitor disease progression
  • Identify response to medications
  • Improve communication with your IBD team
  • Reduce emergency visits by identifying warning signs promptly

When Should You Contact Your IBD Team?

Contact your IBD team if your child develops:
  • Increasing abdominal pain
  • Persistent blood in stool
  • More than 6 stools/day
  • Persistent fever
  • Poor oral intake
  • Weight loss
  • Signs of dehydration
  • Any symptoms that move into the Amber or Red Zone of the Flare Toolkit
