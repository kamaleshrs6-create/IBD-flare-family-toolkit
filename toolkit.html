How to Use These Resources

Symptom Tracker: Use the Google Form to regularly log your child's daily symptoms. This creates a comprehensive record that you can share with your IBD team during appointments.

Stool Chart: The Bristol Stool Scale helps you describe your child's bowel movements accurately. Take photos or screenshots to share with your healthcare provider.

Nutrition Guidelines: Access dietary recommendations and meal planning tips to support your child during flares and remission periods.

Clinical Resources: These evidence-based guidelines provide detailed information about nutrition management in IBD for healthcare professionals and informed parents.
