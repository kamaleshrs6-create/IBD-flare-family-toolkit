Healthy Eating Tips

  • Eat small, frequent meals.
  • Maintain adequate hydration.
  • Avoid foods that worsen symptoms.
  • Continue prescribed nutritional supplements.
  • Do not start restrictive diets without medical advice.

Important Reminder

There is no single diet that works for every child with IBD. Your child's nutrition plan should always be tailored according to disease activity, nutritional status, food tolerance and recommendations from your healthcare team.
